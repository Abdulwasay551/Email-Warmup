1. Introduction

Welcome to {{ settings.app_name }}. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our email warm-up automation service.

By using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you register, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Full name
  • Password (encrypted)

2.2 Gmail OAuth Information

When you connect your Gmail account via Google OAuth, we collect and store:

  • OAuth access tokens (encrypted)
  • OAuth refresh tokens (encrypted)
  • Gmail email address
  • Token expiration timestamps

2.3 Email Activity Data

To provide our warm-up service, we track:

  • Number of emails sent and received
  • Email delivery status
  • Campaign performance metrics
  • Last activity timestamps

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Services: Send and receive emails through your Gmail account for warm-up campaigns
  • Monitor Performance: Track email deliverability and campaign effectiveness
  • Improve Service: Analyze usage patterns to enhance our platform
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Communication: Send service-related notifications and updates

4. Google API Services User Data Policy

{{ settings.app_name }}'s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4.1 Gmail API Scopes

We request the following Gmail API permissions:

  • gmail.send: Send emails on your behalf
  • gmail.readonly: Read emails to monitor responses
  • gmail.modify: Move emails from spam to inbox
  • userinfo.email: Identify your Gmail address

4.2 Limited Use Disclosure

We only use Gmail data for the specific purpose of email warm-up automation. We do not:

  • Read or store your email content beyond what's necessary for the service
  • Share your Gmail data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Transfer data to other apps

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Regular security audits and updates

5.2 Data Storage

Your data is stored in secure PostgreSQL databases with:

  • Encrypted connections (SSL/TLS)
  • Regular automated backups
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Geographic redundancy

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing
  • Service Providers: With trusted providers who help operate our service (database hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your account information and data
  • Modify: Update your account details
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Request a copy of your data
  • Revoke Access: Disconnect Gmail integration at any time

7.2 Revoking Gmail Access

You can revoke our access to your Gmail account at any time by:

  1. Visiting Google Account Permissions
  2. Finding {{ settings.app_name }} in the list
  3. Clicking "Remove Access"

Or by deleting your inbox connection from within our application.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account:
